Who We Are

Tree-Shrew Publishing is an independent publishing house that aims to provide an environment for emerging authors to fully explore their creative ideas, without the shackles of editorial control. It is our mission to ensure that this environment is supportive and encouraging, while remaining truly professional. Tree-Shrew Publishing supplies the facilities and business acumen to turn an author's creative work into a commercial success.

What We Do

Quite simply, we publish books. Tree-Shrew Publishing specialises in publishing both adult and children's fiction novels, however we have also been known to publish works of history or biographies. Over the years Tree-Shrew Publishing has published hundreds of books from emerging authors across the world, all looking to escape the sometimes crushing grasp of editorial control. Our commitment to providing each author with a supportive and nurturing environment, allowing creative ideas to be both realised as well as become a commercial success.
